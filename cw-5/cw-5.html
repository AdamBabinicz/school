Ćwiczenia gramatyczne

Egzamin E8 - zbiór zadań. Parafrazy zdań.
Wykorzystując wyrazy podane dużymi literami w nawiasie, uzupełnij każde zdanie z luką, tak aby zachować sens zdania wyjściowego. Wymagana jest pełna poprawność ortograficzna i gramatyczna wpisywanych fragmentów zdań.

Uzupełnij

Ex.2

1. I often water my tulips and roses in the morning. (BY)
My tulips and roses are me in the morning.

My tulips and roses are watered by me in the morning.

2. He must tell his parents about the accident. (IF) him, I would tell my parents about the accident.

If I were him, I would tell my parents about the accident.

3. This girl is so beautiful. (SUCH)
This is girl.

This is such a beautiful girl.

4. I have got a lot of pencils on the desk. (THERE) a lot of pencils on the desk.

There are a lot of pencils on the desk.

5. This is such a sporty footballer. (SO)
This footballer is

This footballer is so sporty

6. My laptop isn’t cheap, but his is much more expensive. (EXPENSIVE)
My laptop isn’t as his.

My laptop isn’t as expensive as his.

7. We will walk the dog if it doesn’t rain. (UNLESS)
We will walk the dog

We will walk the dog unless it rains.

8. My friends and I had dinner and then we played computer games. (HAD)
We played computer games after my dinner.

We played computer games after my friends and I had dinner.

9. Only Tom got the best mark for the Maths test. (ONLY)
Tom is the got the best mark for the Maths test.

Tom is the only who got the best mark for the Maths test.

10. There aren’t any discos in my district. (NO)
There in my district.

There are no discos in my district.
