Ćwiczenia gramatyczne

Strona Bierna. Ułóż zdania z wyrazów w odpowiedniej kolejności.

Ułóż zdania

Ex.1

1. a letter / John / was / written / by

A letter was written by John.

2. my dog / hit / a car / was / with

My dog was hit with a car.

3. at the moment /a fridge / being / is / cleaned / Martin / by

A fridge is being cleaned by Martin at the moment.

4. last year / a new computer / by / bought / was / my brother

A new computer was bought by my brother last year.

5. by Kate / an interesting novel / being / was / read

An interesting novel was read by Kate.

6. since January / this house / been / has / built / Mr Clarkson /by

This house has been built by Mr Clarkson since January.

7. the bank / occupied / been / had / the terrorist / by

The bank had been occupied by the terrorist.

8. to the hospital / by / you / be / will / taken / my brother

You will be taken to the hospital by my brother.

9. repaired / our windows / by the gold hand / have / will / been

Our windows will have been repaired by the handyman.

10. once a month / tomatoe soup / cooked / is / my sister / by

Tomatoe soup is cooked by my sister once a month.
