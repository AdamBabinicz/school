Ćwiczenia gramatyczne

Present Simple. Zamień zdania w mowie niezależnej na mowę zależną.

Zamień

Ex.1

1. "I am 40 years old" she said.

She said she was 40 years old

2. "I love Maths" he said.

He said he loved Maths.

3. "My sister doesn’t do any sports" Bob said to Tom.

Bob said to Tom his sister didn't do any sports.

4. "Ola lives in Cracow" Ted said.

Ted said Ola lived in Cracow.

5. "We don’t have any money" they said.

They said they didn't have any money.

6. "Do they work late?" they asked me.

They asked me if they worked late.

7. "My sister needs a new guitar" she said

She said her sister needed a new guitar.

8. "The lessons are very interesting" the student said.

The student said the lessons were very interesting.

9. "The TV doesn’t work" he said.

He said the Tv didn't work.

10. "I am bored" she said.

She said she was born.
